About Us

My Vision Match LLC (MVM) was founded in late 2019 by like-minded, successful professionals with the goal of supporting and training disadvantaged groups of our communities, such as women, minorities, immigrants who usually struggle to secure professional jobs, at times despite having college level degrees, through organized, flexible, easy-to-follow beginner level and advanced IT courses . A significant majority of the founders of MVM are IT professionals who understand what it takes to be successful in the field. Guided by our strong mission & vision statements and using top-of-the-field instructors with innovative & advanced, yet flexible and user-friendly instruction methodology, we aspire to become pioneers in the IT training industry. Mission: Our mission is to deliver high quality and competitive IT professionals through unique, online, structured training paths that build strong IT fundamentals and prepare for advanced areas, coupled with student-centered hands-on-training and unwavering pre and post employment support using our expert instructors. Vision: Our vision is to become pioneers in virtual, beginner and advanced level IT training and education targeting in-demand industry certifications, both in the US and abroad, using our exceptional curricular approach, top-of-the-field teachers, and innovative, flexible and latest instruction technologies.
