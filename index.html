Hands-on Internship

During the program, you will be required to remotely login and work with your team member on a project assigned to you at least 4hrs per day, 5 days in a week for the total of 4 weeks (80hrs). We will work to our best to make the program offers flexibility around your personal schedule. The internship program offers the opportunity to work on real world project; the project/s you will work on during the program will help you to:

  1. Gain valuable work experience and confidence.
  2. Develop and refine your skills.
  3. Gain experience in developing real world applications.
  4. Give yourself an edge in the job market.
Besides working on real world projects; during this time you will get a chance to expose yourself and get to know how to use different world class applications and learn to work with project team members.
  1. Applications provided on Microsoft office 365 like Microsoft iterative team management system - teams and email application - outlook
  2. Jira cloud task management system, and a lot more).
  3. You will participate in sprint planning, daily scrum meeting, weekly retrospective meeting, present your final project to a larger team and thereby your work will be evaluated, approved and checked in.

About Us

Who is MVM

My Vision Match LLC (MVM) was founded in late 2019 by like-minded, successful professionals with the goal of supporting and training disadvantaged groups of our communities, such as women, minorities, immigrants who usually struggle to secure professional jobs, at times despite having college level degrees, through organized, flexible, easy-to-follow beginner level and advanced IT courses. A significant majority of the founders of MVM are IT professionals who understand what it takes to be successful in the field. Guided by our strong mission & vision statements and using top-of-the-field instructors with innovative & advanced, yet flexible and user-friendly instruction methodology, we aspire to become pioneers in the IT training industry.

Mission

Our mission is to deliver high quality and competitive IT professionals through unique, online, structured training paths that build strong IT fundamentals and prepare for advanced areas, coupled with student-centered hands-on-training and unwavering pre and post employment support using our expert instructors.

Vision

Our vision is to become pioneers in virtual, beginner and advanced level IT training and education targeting in-demand industry certifications, both in the US and abroad, using our exceptional curricular approach, top-of-the-field teachers, and innovative, flexible and latest instruction technologies.
