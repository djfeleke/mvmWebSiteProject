Summer Camp Registration Guide

Greetings from My Vision Match (MVM)- Your Trusted Professional IT Training Provider! It is our pleasure to invite you to register for the course(s) provided by MVM. We are very excited you chose to train with us, and rest assured, we will work with you based on your needs.

The SUMMER CAMP class will start on June 14 and each course lasts for 4 weeks. Classes will be delivered on Monday - Friday after 6:00pm EST

Please follow the steps provided below to register

  1. Click here Summer Camp and follow the STEPS below
  2. Click on SIGN UP and fill out all the required fields with the the STUDENT information
  3. Use a strong password that is at least 8 characters long and contains at least 1 uppercase letter, 1 lowercase letter, and 1 number. Once you sign up, please wait for some time until your account gets activated by the administrator.
  4. After your account is activated, Goto https://myvisionmatch.talentlms.com/index and click on LOGIN and type your username and password you created on STEP-3.
  5. Please read and ACCEPT our Terms of Service.
  6. Once you successfully login, navigate to the upper right corner of the page. Click on COURSE CATELOG
  7. Check off the checkbox next to SUMMER CAMP COURSE, this will filter out only summer camp courses for you
  8. Click on the 'GET THIS COURSE' button under the course you want to register for
  9. Put in your payment information and click on the CHECK OUT button

This completes your temporary registration. You will get a separate email notification for orientation date. If you have any questions, do not hesitate to contact us.

Regards!
